Demon Days

The second studio album by Gorillaz

A New Kind of Hip-Hop, Rock, and Pop

"Feel Good Inc." is a perfect fusion of alternative rock and hip-hop, featuring the iconic laugh and bassline from Gorillaz.
The track is elevated by the energetic and chaotic rap verses from legendary hip-hop group De La Soul.

Interesting Fact: "Feel Good Inc." won a Grammy for Best Pop Collaboration with Vocals in 2006 and topped Billboard's Alternative Songs chart for eight consecutive weeks.
